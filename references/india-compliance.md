# India Compliance: DLT, 140 / 160-Series Numbers

To make commercial or telemarketing calls to Indian mobile/landline numbers, you must use **regulated phone numbers** registered on the TRAI **DLT** (Distributed Ledger Technology) platform. This applies to both promotional and transactional calls.

| Number series | Use case | Bolna telephony provider |
|---|---|---|
| **140-series** | Promotional / telemarketing | Vobiz |
| **160-series** | Transactional / service (BFSI, alerts, OTP follow-ups, etc.) | Plivo |

DLT registration is mandatory before either series can be procured. Plan **2-4 weeks** from start to first call.

---

## 140-series (Vobiz)

### Process

1. **Register as Principal Entity on DLT** at the [TATA Teleservices DLT portal](https://telemarketer.tatateleservices.com/).
2. **Complete digital KYC** with these documents:

   | Document | Source |
   |---|---|
   | Certificate of Incorporation | MCA / Registrar of Companies |
   | GST Certificate | GST portal |
   | Company PAN | Income tax |
   | Director List + MOA | Internal records |
   | Signed Letter of Authorization (LOA) | Director listed in MOA must sign |

   Email `compliance@bolna.ai` for the LOA template.

3. **Submit LOA** with the official mobile number and email for OTP verification. **These become permanent registered contacts** — they cannot be easily changed later. Use a long-lived ops mailbox, not a personal one.
4. **Pay ₹5,900** on the portal-generated payment link.
5. Wait for DLT to issue your **PE ID** and **TM ID**.

---

## 160-series (Plivo)

### Extra requirements beyond DLT

| Document | Purpose |
|---|---|
| RBI / SEBI certificate | Proof of regulatory compliance during Header registration. |
| Certificate of Incorporation + GST | Plivo KYC. |
| PE ID + TM ID | Generated by DLT. |
| Compliance Application Name | Shared with Plivo for provisioning. |

### Process

1. **Complete DLT registration** (same as 140-series above). Generates PE ID + TM ID.
2. **Submit KYC to Plivo** via Bolna (skip if Plivo KYC already done).
3. **Share PE ID + TM ID + compliance application name** with Bolna.
4. **Plivo allocates the 160-series number**. The number is **not active** at this stage.
5. **Header registration on DLT** with the RBI/SEBI certificate.
6. **Obtain URN** (Unique Reference Number) after Header is approved. Share URN and approval screenshot with Bolna.
7. **Template registration on DLT** once Header is approved.
8. **Numbers become active** once Template is approved.

## Common gotchas

- **Different KYC for Plivo vs Vobiz**. Plivo KYC isn't valid for Vobiz and vice versa — both go through Bolna ops.
- **LOA contacts are permanent**. Don't put a personal mobile/email here.
- **OTPs are time-sensitive**. Keep the registered phone reachable throughout the process.
- **160-series numbers stay inactive until Template approval**. Don't promise customers a go-live date earlier than that.
- **DLT charges may change**. ₹5,900 quoted above is current at time of writing; verify on the DLT portal.
- **Existing Plivo KYC** can be reused — surface this so users don't redo KYC unnecessarily.

For up-to-date specifics, contact `compliance@bolna.ai`.
